# What’s new in Claude: Turning Claude into your thinking partner

Over the past several weeks, we've released features that help Claude transform your most creative ideas into finished work. Here's how.

Over the past several weeks, we've released features that help Claude transform your most creative ideas into finished work. Here's how.

- November 20, 2025
- 5min

Your best work doesn't happen all at once. It starts as a rough idea, then takes shape through conversation, iteration, and building.

We’ve shipped a lot of new features in Claude over the past few months—memory, voice, file creation, and more. Together, they add up to something bigger: Claude as a thinking partner. Now, Claude isn’t just answering questions but staying with you through the messy process of thinking and building. Here’s how it works.

A good thinking partner doesn't make you start from scratch every time. Claude now keeps track of your preferences, projects, and working style with memory, so context carries forward across conversations. Tell Claude you prefer prose over bullet points, or that you're planning a Q1 launch, and it'll remember.

Sometimes you need to talk through an idea before it's ready to write down. Voice mode lets you have natural conversations with Claude on mobile, for brainstorming, quick questions, or working through problems. Dictation is available on mobile and desktop when you'd rather talk than type.

A thinking partner isn't useful if it's stuck in one place. Claude now meets you where you're working: on mobile to draft messages, create calendar invites, or make plans using your location.

In your browser with Claude for Chrome to handle forms, extract data, and automate repetitive steps. And connected to tools like Notion, Canva, Slack, Linear, Figma, and more so Claude can pull in context and take action directly.

When you're ready to build, Claude can run analysis, create polished documents, spreadsheets, and presentations, or help you prototype websites and interactive tools right in the conversation.

No matter where you start, Claude helps you get it done.
